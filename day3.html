Day 3 — Glutes + Arms

lift & define

Goal: hit glutes for the second time this week while adding focused arm work for definition. Keep the glute exercises controlled and the arm work strict instead of using momentum.

10 exercises Glutes + arms focus 70–95 min

Workout Cards
This session should feel glute-dominant, not quad-dominant. On arm work, keep the reps smooth and controlled with full range of motion.

Smith Machine Glute Bridge or Hip Thrust

4 sets10–12 reps90 sec rest
Cues

Strong squeeze at the top, controlled lowering, and keep ribs down.

Progression

When all sets hit 12 clean reps, increase weight.

Bulgarian Split Squat, Glute-Biased

3 sets8–10 each side75–90 sec rest
Cues

Take a longer step, lean slightly forward, and push through the heel to shift emphasis to the glutes.

Progression
