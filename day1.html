Day 1 — Glutes + Hamstrings

build the base

Goal: grow glutes while keeping quad growth moderate. Keep tension on glutes and hamstrings, use controlled form, and avoid turning the session into a heavy quad day.

Workout Cards
Complete all working sets with good form. Most sets should finish with 1–2 reps left in reserve.

Barbell Hip Thrust

4 sets8–12 reps90–120 sec rest
Cues

Full lockout, pause 1 second at the top, keep ribs down, and control the lowering.

Progression

When all 4 sets reach 12 clean reps, increase the weight.

Romanian Deadlift

3 sets8–10 reps90–120 sec rest
Cues

Slight knee bend, hinge hips back, keep a neutral spine, and lower slowly.

Progression

Add reps first, then increase weight.

Cable Glute Kickback

3 sets12–15 each side45–60 sec rest
Cues

Do not swing, squeeze the glute at end range, and control the return.

Progression

Reach 15 reps cleanly, then increase load.

3 sets12–15 reps60 sec rest
Cues

Drive hips into the pad, squeeze glutes at the top, and move with control.

Progression

Cues

Cues

Take a longer step, keep a slight torso lean, and push through the heel.
