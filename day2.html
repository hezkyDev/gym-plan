Day 2 — Back + Shoulders + Core

shape the frame

Goal: build upper-body shape to enhance the waist-to-shoulder ratio. Focus on posture, lat width, shoulder shape, and core control rather than rushing heavy weights.

10 exercises + cardio Upper body shape 75–100 min

Use clean, controlled reps. Most sets should finish with 1–2 reps left in reserve, especially on shoulder work and core movements.

Lat Pulldown

4 sets10–12 reps75–90 sec rest
Cues

Pull elbows down, keep chest up, and avoid swinging.

Progression

When all sets hit 12 clean reps, increase weight.
